Ham and Cheese Pannini

Ingredients

  • 2 panini bread rolls (or 4 slices of your favorite bread)
  • 85 grams smoked cheddar
  • 4 large slices of crumbed ham
  • 16 baby plum tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 2 tbsp of dijon mustard
  • a handful of arugula (rocket)

Instructions

  1. First, in a pan, over medium-high heat, fry your tomatoes for 3-4 minutes in a drizzle of olive oil to give them some delicious caramelization.

  2. Next, assemble your panini. For each panini, spread 1 tbsp of mustard on one half of the panini, followed by a few slices of folded ham.

  3. On the other half, lay down a couple of slices of cheddar. Add the roasted baby tomatoes, and top the tomatoes with a handful of arugula.
